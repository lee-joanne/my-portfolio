About Me

I’m a UI/UX Designer with a background in Full Stack Software Development. Currently pursuing Google's UX Design Professional Certificate.

After earning my degree in Applied Sciences from the University of Guelph, I moved to Ireland on a working holiday visa. While traveling across Europe and taking on various roles, I eventually landed a position in Apple’s Siri department, an experience that sparked my passion for technology. Motivated by this interest, I self-funded a Diploma in Full Stack Software Development at Code Institute.

After returning to Vancouver, I joined a startup as a Full Stack Developer with UI/UX responsibilities. There, I discovered how fulfilling it is to blend my psychology background with technical skills to create thoughtful, user-centred interfaces, which solidified my passion for UI/UX design.

Outside of tech, I enjoy playing the piano, painting, drawing, going to concerts, and travelling.

This portfolio was crafted from the ground up using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to reflect my skills in both UI/UX design and front-end development.

– Based in Vancouver, BC
