Meet the Designer

UI/UX Designer with Full Stack Expertise.

After graduating from the University of Guelph with a degree in Applied Sciences, I moved abroad to Ireland in search of my passion. I was fortunate to join Apple’s European Headquarters, working on the Siri team to enhance the product’s responsiveness to user needs. This experience sparked my interest in tech, leading me to pursue a Diploma in Full Stack Software Development with a specialization in Advanced Front-End. Following five years in Ireland, I returned to Vancouver, where I worked as a technical tutor for Code Institute and later took on a Full Stack Developer role at Inkeros, with a strong focus on UI/UX. Through these experiences, I discovered a deep passion for human-centred design and found my true calling in UI/UX design.

Outside of tech, I enjoy playing the piano, cozy gaming, illustrating, and traveling. I'm passionate about supporting others and love volunteering to help create safe, inclusive spaces within the community.

This portfolio was hand-coded with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to showcase my passion for clean, intuitive interfaces and front-end development.

– Based in Vancouver, BC

My Mission & Values

Ensuring inclusive, equitable, and accessible design for everyone.
