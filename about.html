About Me

UI/UX Designer with a Full Stack Development background, currently pursuing Google’s UX Design Certificate to deepen my expertise.

With a background in Applied Sciences and early tech experience at Apple’s Siri department, I quickly found my calling in UI/UX. After earning a Diploma in Full Stack Software Development from Code Institute, I returned to Vancouver and dove deeper into design, supporting students as a tutor and later taking on UI/UX-heavy responsibilities as a Full Stack Developer at Inkeros.

This portfolio was hand-coded with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to showcase my passion for clean, intuitive interfaces and front-end development.

Outside of tech, I love playing piano, painting, illustrating, and travelling.

– Based in Vancouver, BC
