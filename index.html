- About Me -

Hello! I am a Canadian from a small city called Sarnia in Ontario, currently living in Vancouver. Before Vancouver, I lived in Ireland for five years and worked in Quality in the AIML department at Apple which introduced me to the tech industry. I decided to do Code Institute's Diploma in Full Stack Development with Advanced Front-End Specialization and now work for Code Institute as a Software Developer & Learning Success Tutor. I am building my skills in debugging and complex problem-solving by helping to solve a large volume of student projects per week. I am currently looking to expand my skills and apply them in a more project-delivery-focused role so I am looking for Junior Full Stack Developer or Web Developer positions.

In my spare time outside of coding, I like to paint, draw, journal, listen to reverby music, take photographs, and play vintage Nintendo consoles. I also enjoy ice skating, swimming, hiking, bike riding, and learning new musical instruments.
