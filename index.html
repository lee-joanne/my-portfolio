<h1>I'm Joanne, a Full Stack Software Developer</h1>

- About Me -

Hello! I'm a Canadian currently residing in Vancouver. Prior to moving here, I spent five years living in Ireland, where I worked in Quality within the AIML department at Apple. This experience introduced me to the tech industry, prompting my decision to pursue Code Institute's Diploma in Full Stack Development with Advanced Front-End Specialization. I now work at Inkeros as a Full Stack Software Developer. In my current role, I am responsible for designing and planning the company's new platform, and transforming these concepts into functional software. I use Figma for wireframing and Django as the primary framework for development.

In my free time, apart from coding, I enjoy creating illustrations, painting, journaling, listening to shoegaze/dreampop, collecting vinyl records, vintage gaming, and taking film photographs. I also like to go hiking in local spots and explore the city on weekends. Currently, I am interested in knitting, Nintendo games, film photography, and aspiring to pursue my dream of making my own Pokemon Emerald ROM hack.
